Module 1

Cybercrime is defined in simple words as a crime that is done online.

Here, the medium used to commit crime digitally is the computer, network, internet, or any electronic device. The main targets of cybercrime are users of the system, websites, company defamation, gaining money, etc. Some of the activities of cyber-criminals are listed below: Spread viruses and malware to cause harm to computers and sensitive data. Attacks a computer to reach the target or victim’s computer via network. Hack the victim’s system and steals confidential information from the user’s data. Gaining unauthorized access to user accounts. Paving ways for online scams and frauds. Generate profit by selling or locking crucial data.
